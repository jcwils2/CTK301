My passion - Elevator pitch

Through video games I created connections and devolped into the charcter that I am today from the games that my older brother had me playing. I will always say this when asked what is my major and why, is that I had planned to not waste more of my life attending school. As far as it goes I am an older child so I have to set the example, so I did my research and decided that I was going to do something that I already have a deep love for. I also looked at the adults in my life and how I found their lives a little bit miserable and the outlier was that their career had sucked all the joy out of it.
